Global Video Game Sales Over the Years

// About This Chart

This interactive line chart displays the total global video game sales (in millions of units) from 1980 to 2016, with each point representing the combined sales across all titles released that year. Hover over any point to see the exact sales figure. Use the Genre dropdown to filter the chart by game genre.

// Key Takeaway

The data reveals a clear story of explosive industry growth followed by decline. Sales were modest and inconsistent through the 1980s and early 1990s, before a sharp inflection point around 1996 when yearly sales more than doubled, likely driven by the adoption of the PlayStation and Nintendo 64. Growth continued through the 2000s, peaking at approximately 630 million units in 2008, before declining steadily each year through 2016 as the industry shifted toward digital distribution and mobile gaming.
