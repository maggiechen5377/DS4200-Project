NA Sales vs EU Sales by Genre

// About This Chart

This interactive scatter plot compares North American sales and European sales for individual video games. Each point represents one game and is colored by genre. Use the global sales slider to filter the chart to games below a chosen worldwide sales threshold, helping reduce clutter and focus on specific market segments.

// Key Takeaway

There is a general positive relationship, meaning games selling well in North America also tend to sell well in Europe. Notable exceptions exist: some games are heavily skewed toward North America (NBA titles and other sports with limited overseas appeal), while others perform better in Europe (FIFA, which is extremely popular in European markets). Shooters and Action games cluster near the top right, confirming their strength in both western regions.
