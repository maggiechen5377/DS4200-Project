// Project Overview

Video games are very popular all over the world, and one key metric companies use to measure the success of their games is sales. The video game industry has grown into a global entertainment powerhouse, generating over $180 billion annually and reaching billions of players worldwide.

This project analyzes video game sales data to uncover patterns in consumer behavior and market dynamics across different regions, genres, publishers, and time periods. By visualizing sales trends, we aim to identify what drives commercial success in gaming and how regional preferences vary globally.

// Further Reading

This analysis provides valuable insights for industry stakeholders making strategic decisions about game development and marketing, while also revealing the cultural and economic forces shaping one of the world's fastest-growing entertainment sectors.

Further reading on video game sales analysis can be found at VGChartz and at the Kaggle dataset page.
