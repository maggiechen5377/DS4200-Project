Publisher to Genre Flow: Top 8 Publishers

// About This Chart

This Sankey diagram shows the relationship between the top 8 publishers (by game count) and the genres they produce. The thickness of each flow represents the number of games that publisher produced in that genre. Hover over any node or link to see the exact count. Publishers are shown on the left and genres on the right.

// Key Takeaway

Electronic Arts creates the most games overall, with the thickest flow going into the Sports genre, indicating that EA dominates sports game production. Action and Sports are the most popular genres across all top publishers, as reflected by their thick outgoing flows. Genres like Puzzle, Adventure, and Strategy have noticeably thinner flows, meaning publishers produce comparatively fewer titles in those categories.
