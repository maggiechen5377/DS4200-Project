Video Game Sales by Genre and Region

// About This Chart

This interactive horizontal stacked bar chart displays cumulative sales (in millions) for each video game genre, with bars subdivided into four color-coded segments representing North America, Europe, Japan, and Other regions. Click any genre bar to highlight it and dim the rest. Hover over a segment for exact sales figures and regional percentages.

// Key Takeaway

North America consistently dominates across nearly every genre, often accounting for 40 to 50 percent of total sales. Action is the top-selling genre by a wide margin, followed by Sports and Shooter. Japan is a notable exception, showing a disproportionately strong presence in Role-Playing games, reflecting a cultural affinity for the format rooted in franchises like Final Fantasy and Dragon Quest. Genres such as Puzzle, Strategy, and Adventure have comparatively limited global reach.
