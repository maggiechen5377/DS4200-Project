NA Sales vs EU Sales by Genre

// About This Chart

This interactive scatter plot compares North American sales and European sales for all video games in the dataset. Each point represents one game and is colored by genre. Use the global sales slider to filter to games below a chosen worldwide sales threshold. Hover over any point for details.

// Key Takeaway

There is a general positive relationship, meaning games selling well in North America also tend to sell well in Europe. Notable exceptions exist: some games are heavily skewed toward North America (NBA titles and American sports franchises), while others perform better in Europe (FIFA, which is extremely popular in European markets). Shooters and Action games cluster near the top right, confirming their strength in both western regions.
